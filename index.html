Bringing Fun Test Drive Experiences & Excitement to You

Toyota Go Tour is on the move with new surprises for everyone. There will be exciting test-drives of our full range of models, irresistible deals, great games and snacks for kids, enticing contest and more. It’s a road show you do not want to miss!

Locate a Road Tour Near You

What’s Happening

There are tons to do for the whole family as soon as you enter. Here’s a sneak peek of some excitement you can expect.

  • Test-Drive Your Way

    Choose to test-drive on any road type with our selected models (Vios, Corolla Altis, Camry, Hilux*, Fortuner, Sienta) and discover how we will redefine your drive.

  • Feel the Adrenaline Rush

    Take on this test-drive with our new Hilux on a thrilling obstacle course.

  • Catch Great Deals

    Discover our host of irresistible offers when you purchase a Toyota at our road tour.

  • Enter the Fun Zone

    This kid-friendly zone is where sweet treats and kid-friendly games are freely available.

  • Relax and Unwind

    Sit down and enjoy a cup of coffee while our friendly sales representative answer your inquiries.

  • Hilux Buy and Win

    Drive home a new Hilux and stand a chance to win** a brand new 7-seater Sienta!
